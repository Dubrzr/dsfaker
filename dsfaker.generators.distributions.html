class dsfaker.generators.distributions.Beta(a: typing.Union[float, numpy.ndarray, typing.Iterable[float]], b: typing.Union[float, numpy.ndarray, typing.Iterable[float]], seed=None)[source]¶

Bases: dsfaker.generators.base.DistributionBounded

The Beta distribution is bounded and continuous.

The implementation is from numpy.random.mtrand.RandomState.beta.

Distribution function:

\[f(x; a,b) = \frac{1}{B(\alpha, \beta)} x^{\alpha - 1} (1 - x)^{\beta - 1}\]

where the normalisation, B, is the beta function,

\[B(\alpha, \beta) = \int_0^1 t^{\alpha - 1} (1 - t)^{\beta - 1} dt.\]
